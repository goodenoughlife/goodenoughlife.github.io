Background

Since 2018, I have been on a mission to accomplish a (seemingly) simple thing: live a good enough life. After a childhood filled with trauma and teenage years dominated by self harm and suicidal ideation, I found myself, 21, in a predicament; I WANTED to live, I had good friends and I had a potential career path I was excited about, but bouts of severe anxiety, dread, self-doubt, inability to focus, paranoia, and intense mood swings had overtaken my day to day life and I was clueless of how to get better. I dedicated most of 2019 to stabilizing through psychodynamic therapy, meditation, yoga, fellowship, and reading psychology, sociology and neuroscience literature. In 2020 during the global pandemic, I entered a relationship that helped bring my stability to new heights and I paused my healing journey. Fast foward to the end of 2023, and that relationship ended in a situation mirroring the trauma of my childhood, and I found myself unstable again for the first time in years. This website is documentation of part 2 of my healing journey.
